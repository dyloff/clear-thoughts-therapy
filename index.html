My Story

My name is Richard Caldwell and I am a partner of City and West Therapy Ltd.   I am a qualified counsellor and psychotherapist and registered member of the British Association of Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP)*, and with my knowledge and experience I can provide a warm, safe environment and an approach that is simple and down to earth.

I come from an earlier career in the travel industry which has given me a significant insight in to different cultures and religions, and has allowed me to gain a broad experience of life and its demands. This I feel is important in order to engage with this fast, and sometimes chaotic world we live in.

My Qualiifcations

  • BACP - British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy Accredited Member
  • Level 5 - Diploma in Cognitive Behavioural Therapeutic Skills and Theory
  • Level 4 - Diploma in Therapeutic Counselling
  • Level 3 - Certificate in Counselling Studies
  • Level 2 - Certificate in Counselling Skills
  • DBS - Disclosure and Baring Service Certificate

Therapy & You

Whether seeking Online or In-Person therapy its natural to explore this website and ask yourself if counselling and talking to someone will help? Let me try answer this for you.

To make therapy work for you can depend on your feelings about therapy generally, what you want help with, and the relationship between you and your therapist. I believe the relationship is paramount, and in addition to this my role is to empower you to find your own answers in a safe and confidential environment.

I work with individuals on an open-ended basis or for an agreed time period, with the aim of enabling you to enhance your life and to live it with a fuller zest of confidence and peace.

Just to re-emphasise, all discussions will be in confidence.
